Helpers

Center Device Horizontally

Give the device the class of "cd-center" 
<div class="cd-mobile cd-5s-silver cd-center">
  ...
</div>

Fill screen of the device with an image, iframe, or any other element.

Give the element inside the screen the class of "cd-fill" 
<div class="cd-mobile cd-5s-silver">
  ...
  <div class="cd-screen">
    <img class="cd-fill" src="location/of/image.png">
  </div>
</div>
